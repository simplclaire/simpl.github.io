RO WOON (로운)


BIOGRAPHY


Kim Seok-woo (born August 7, 1996), better known by his stage name Rowoon, is a South Korean singer and actor. He is a member of the K-pop boy band SF9. He played his first leading role in 2019 MBC's television series, Extraordinary You .
