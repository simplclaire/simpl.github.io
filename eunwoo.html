CHA EUN WOO (차은우)


BIOGRAPHY


Lee Dong-min (Korean: 이동민; Hanja: 車銀優; born March 30, 1997), professionally known as Cha Eun-woo (차은우), is a South Korean singer, actor, and model under the label of Fantagio. He is a member of the South Korean boy group Astro.
