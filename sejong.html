YANG SE JONG (양세종)


BIOGRAPHY


Yang Se-jong (born December 23, 1992) is a South Korean actor and model. Yang become well known when he starred as a young chef in the 2017 romance drama Temperature of Love which won him three Best New Actor awards at the SBS Drama Awards, Baeksang Arts Awards, and the 6th APAN Star Awards. His other works included Dr. Romantic (2016), Duel (2017), Still 17 (2018) & My Country: The New Age (2019).
