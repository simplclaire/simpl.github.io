YOON SHI YOON (윤시윤)


BIOGRAPHY


Yoon Shi-yoon (Korean: 윤시윤; born Yoon Dong-gu on September 26, 1986)[1] is a South Korean actor and television personality. He is best known for his leading roles in King of Baking , Kim Takgu (2010), My Cute Guys (2013), Hit the Top (2017), Grand Prince (2018) and Your Honor (2018). The Nokdu Flower (2019), Psychopath Diary (2019-2020). In 2016, he became a member of the variety show 2 Days & 1 Night .
