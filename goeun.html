KIM GO EUN (김고은)


BIOGRAPHY


Kim Go-eun (Korean: 김고은; Hanja: 金高銀; born July 2, 1991) is a South Korean actress and singer. She debuted in the film A Muse (2012) where she won several Best New Actress awards in South Korea. She is also known for her role in the television series Cheese in the Trap (2016), Guardian: The Lonely and Great God (2016), and The King: Eternal Monarch (2020).
