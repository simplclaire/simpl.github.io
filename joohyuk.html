NAM JOO HYUK (남주혁)


BIOGRAPHY


Nam Joo-hyuk (born February 22, 1994) is a South Korean model and actor. He has starred in Who Are You: School 2015 (2015), Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok-joo (2016), The Bride of Habaek (2017) and Dazzling (2019).
