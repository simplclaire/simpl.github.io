AHN HYO SEOP (안효섭)


BIOGRAPHY


Ahn Hyo-seop (born April 17, 1995) is a Canadian-Korean actor and singer based in South Korea.[1][2] He is a member of the project group One O One formed by Starhaus Entertainment. He gained recognition for playing Seo Woo-jin in the second season of the hit Korean medical drama, Dr. Romantic 2 alongside Lee Sung-kyung and Han Suk-kyu .
