KIM SEON HO (김선호)


BIOGRAPHY


Kim Seon-ho is a South Korean actor under Salt Entertainment. He debuted as an actor in 2009, with his first part in a theatrical production and took his first television role in early 2017 with KBS drama Good Manager as Sun Sang-tae. Since then, he has appeared in both variety shows and television dramas, including You Drive Me Crazy (2018), Welcome to Waikiki 2 (2019), 100 Days My Prince (2019), and as a fixed cast member of 2 Days & 1 Night (2019).
